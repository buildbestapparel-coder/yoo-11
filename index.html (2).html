Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity?
MOQ depends on product type, fabric and customization method. Share your requirements and we will give you an accurate answer for your specific order.
How long does production take?
Standard turnaround is 2 weeks after order confirmation and sample approval. Time may vary with quantity, customization method and shipping destination.
Do you offer free samples?
Yes — confirm a bulk order of at least 30 pieces and we will produce a free sample for you to approve quality before full production begins.
Do you ship worldwide?
Yes. We ship worldwide through UPS, DHL, FedEx and Aramex. We handle all packaging, documentation and export coordination for international orders.
What file formats do you accept for designs?
AI, PDF, PNG, PSD, SVG and high quality reference images. If you only have a rough idea, our design team can build a techpack from scratch.
Do you do private label and OEM production?
Yes — we specialize in OEM and private label manufacturing. Custom tags, labels, hangtags and packaging so every piece ships under your brand identity.
